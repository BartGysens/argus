**Description:
Document generator is part of the backoffice system.
It adds the ability to generate documents based upon data in your system.
The documents are in Word, Excel of PDF-format and can be reproduced as many
times as needed.

This module integrates in many different modules such as Stages, LVS, Inschrijvingen, ...

**Benefits:
Generate documents from your data.
