**Description:
Klassenraden is specifically built for managing your meetings about your pupils.
It contains a full system for handling all actions within the process of classmeetings.
Your employees can read their actions which have to been worked out as follow up for
the decisions made.

**Benefits:
Manage your classmeetings.
