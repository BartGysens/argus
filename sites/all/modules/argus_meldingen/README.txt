**Description:
Meldingen is used for bringing behaviour, problems and other needfull information
about your pupils in the system. It describes all sorts of facts where upon your
procedures can reply according to your work systems.

Use this module in conjunction with Smartschool import if in use of.

**Benefits:
Register all the facts about your pupils.
