**Description:
SODA is specially developed for use with the system of evaluating attitudes and
behavior for students. It's designed by scientific and educational research.
More information: https://sodajobs.be/

**Important notice:
Documents used in this module are made upon example delivered by SODA jobs.

**Benefits:
Get controle over your students progress in attitude and behavior.
