**Description:
With the module Stage you can fully administer your external working periods for
your students. You can create chronological timeperiodes, relationships between a
company and a student, reports and contracts as defined by the government.
All documents can be downloaded for print.

**Important notice:
Documents used in this module are made upon example delivered by governement services
of eductation, ref. http://data-onderwijs.vlaanderen.be/edulex/document.aspx?docid=14891.

**Benefits:
Organise your internships.
