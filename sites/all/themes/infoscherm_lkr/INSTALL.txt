**Installation:

Theme Infoscherm Leerkrachten is a theme form the argus - a Quality control extension.

1. Unpack the argus_theme_infoscherm_lkr folder and contents in the appropriate themes
directory of your Drupal installation.  This is probably sites/all/themes/
2. Enable the argus_theme_infoscherm_lkr theme in the administration tools.
3. Please check settings at admin/appearance/settings.
